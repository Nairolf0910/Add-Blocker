=== FULL DESCRIPTION ===

🛡️ Redirect Guard – Stop Unwanted Redirects Before They Happen

Ever clicked a link and ended up on a scam site, an ad popup, or a sketchy domain you never intended to visit? Redirect Guard stops exactly that — before you even notice.

✨ WHAT IT DOES

The moment a suspicious redirect is detected, the extension closes the tab instantly and automatically. No waiting, no clicking — it’s just gone.

✅ PROTECTS AGAINST:
• Scam & phishing redirect chains
• Ad networks & click farms (ouo.io, linkvertise, adfly & 20+ more)
• JavaScript-based redirects (location.assign, location.replace)
• History API hijacking (pushState / replaceState)
• Suspicious TLDs (.top, .xyz, .click, .buzz ...)
• Unwanted popup tabs opened by ad scripts

🔒 100% PRIVATE:
• Zero data collection, no tracking, no external servers
• All settings stored locally on your device only
• Open source – inspect the code yourself

⚙️ SIMPLE TO USE:
• One click to enable/disable
• 3 sensitivity levels (Low / Medium / High)
• Whitelist for trusted domains
• Stats showing how many redirects were blocked today
• Blocked pages can still be opened if you choose to

🚫 HOW IT WORKS:
Redirect Guard monitors every navigation in your browser. When it detects a known scam pattern or suspicious redirect, it closes the tab immediately. If it’s the only open tab, a clean info page is shown instead.

Compatible with Chrome and Microsoft Edge.
